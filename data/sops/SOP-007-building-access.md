# Building in Basel — Campus Access, Dining, and Facilities

A summary of Basel-specific campus topics that may be helpful for you.

## Access to the Building in Basel

The Building in Basel is accessible **24/7 for all internal employees and
approved corporate contractors** using a personal electronic identification card
(**personal ID badge**). External guests and visitors may only enter during the
standard operational hours of the main reception, or via authorized special
permission granted by designated zone managers.

### Primary entry points

> Note: the source document states "five primary entry points" but lists six.
> All six are reproduced below as written.

1. **Main Reception Access:** ground floor; 24/7 via personal ID badge. Visitors
   are admitted strictly during reception opening hours.
2. **Sublevel Parking Access:** first basement (opposite the central car parking
   elevators); 24/7 via personal ID badge through the security revolving door.
3. **Side Avenue Access:** ground floor (side street access point); not on a
   regular schedule and requires special operational authorization.
4. **Bicycle Facility Access:** first basement (from the secure bicycle parking);
   24/7 via personal ID badge through the revolving door.
5. **North Campus Plaza Access:** ground floor (near the internal dining
   facility); free thoroughfare during standard daytime hours, transitioning to
   24/7 personal ID badge verification during off-hours.
6. **Logistics & Loading Zone Access:** secure sliding doors between the transit
   blocks on the ground floor; free access during standard warehouse shipping
   hours or 24/7 via personal ID badge.

### Key access node designations

- **Node A (Main Reception Gate):** 24/7 for staff badge holders. Visitors must
  register here during posted daytime business hours.
- **Node B (North Internal Entrance):** connects to the internal corporate plaza;
  fully automated, accessible 24/7 via personal ID badge.
- **Node C (Dining Facility Entrance):** connects the plaza to the central
  cafeteria. Unlocked during standard meal service hours; no ID verification is
  required for staff during these times.
- **Node D (Peripheral Revolving Door):** secondary perimeter access point from
  the side street. Personal ID badge authentication required 24/7.

## Dining, Networking, and Event Spaces

- **Primary dining locations:** two full-service staff cafeterias — one on the
  ground floor of the Primary Lab Block, one on the top floor of the Adjacent
  Research Tower.
- **Express catering:** a "Grab and Go" kiosk at the main entrance plaza.
- **Networking hubs:** the central Science Club Lounge.
- **Neighborhood break rooms:** localized "marketplace" breakout zones on the
  central level of each workspace hub, with a tea kitchen and coffee station.
- **Event & function spaces:** the bar area within the Recharge & Collaboration
  Zone for tailored events; the campus Main Auditorium for formal banquets and
  large presentations.
