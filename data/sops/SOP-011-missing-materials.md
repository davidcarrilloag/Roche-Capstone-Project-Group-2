# Requesting Missing or Replacement Materials & Peripherals

Use this guide when something is missing, used up, lost or broken and you need a
replacement. Pick the row that matches the item.

## Where to request what

| You need… | Where to go | How |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| Lab consumables (pipette tips, Petri dishes, nitrile gloves, vials, glassware) | **NexusBuy**, or the on-site **Lab Shop** via **LabLend & Go** | Most are already stocked in your **Floor Logistics Zone** — check there first |
| Chemicals, reagents, antibodies | **ChemVantage** | Links to the global SDS automatically |
| IT peripherals & accessories (adapters, cables, mouse, keyboard, headset, monitor, docking station) | **IT Tech Bar** (ground floor, Primary Lab Block, 09:00–12:00 & 13:00–16:00) | Walk in, or raise a **hardware request ticket** in ServiceNow |
| Temporary/borrowed equipment (calibrated sensors, portable vacuum pumps) | **LabLend & Go** | Book and return through the app |
| Replacement personal ID badge (lost/damaged) | **Campus Security Desk** | Report immediately so the old badge is deactivated |
| Specialised / non-catalog / bulk items | Sourcing team | Email `supplyhub@logistics.com` with a technical spec |

## Notes

- **Same-day delivery:** submit digital orders (NexusBuy / ChemVantage) before
  **13:00**; items route via the automated conveyor (08:00–16:00) to your Floor
  Logistics Zone. You receive a routing email from `supplyhub@logistics.com` when
  dispatched.
- **Broken instrument or device** (not just a missing accessory)? That's a
  support/repair case — see *Troubleshooting Common IT Issues & Opening a Support
  Ticket* and *Laboratory Equipment Maintenance & Support*.
- **Approvals:** orders above your limit route to your line manager (NexusBuy) or
  the Lab Manager & EHS (ChemVantage) automatically.
