# New Employee Onboarding & Digital Access Guide

Welcome to the Basel Research Team! As a new researcher or technician at the
Basel campus, setting up your digital workspace and securing the appropriate
software permissions is your top priority during your first week. This guide
outlines the mandatory applications you must request access to, their purpose,
and where to submit the permissions workflow.

## 1. Mandatory Core Applications & Permissions

To request permissions, log into the **Unified Access Portal (UAP)** and search
for the specific catalog ID listed below.

| Application | Purpose / Function | Approval required by | Where to request |
|-------------|--------------------|----------------------|------------------|
| **ChemVantage** | Ordering lab chemicals, synthetic compounds, antibodies; tracking global Safety Data Sheets (SDS). | Lab Manager & EHS | UAP → Catalog ID: `CHEMVAN-01` |
| **NexusBuy** | Sourcing non-chemical consumables (pipette tips, Petri dishes, gloves, vials) and office stationery. | Department Budget Holder | UAP → Catalog ID: `NEX-BUY-PRO` |
| **LabLend & Go** | Self-service on-site Lab Shop purchases, stockroom QR access, booking temporary shared equipment. | Direct Supervisor | Corporate mobile app, or UAP → ID: `LEND-GO-05` |
| **SLS Core** (Smart Laboratory Suite) | Centralized calendar for scheduling shared workbenches and reserving high-value analytical instruments. | Area Zone Manager | SLS Portal → Settings → Access Profile |
| **BioLIMS v4** (Lab Info Management System) | Mandatory platform for logging experimental results, sample tracking, and equipment deviation logs. | Lead Data Integrity Officer | UAP → Catalog ID: `LIMS-BIO-PROD` |
| **ELN Cloud** (Electronic Lab Notebook) | Secure digital notebook for intellectual property recording and formal scientific documentation. | Head of R&D Operations | UAP → Catalog ID: `ELN-NOTE-SEC` |

## 2. Step-by-Step Access Request Workflow

Follow these sequential steps on **Day 2** of your onboarding:

1. **Step 2.1 — Complete safety prerequisites:** before requesting access to
   ChemVantage and BioLIMS v4, complete the mandatory *"Chemical Hygiene and Data
   Integrity"* e-learning modules on the corporate training portal.
2. **Step 2.2 — Submit to the UAP:** navigate to `uap.basel-campus.internal`,
   copy the corresponding Catalog ID from the table above, and click
   *"Request Access"*.
3. **Step 2.3 — Manager notification:** an automated notification is sent to your
   supervisor. Inform your Lab Manager so they can review and release the
   approval line.
4. **Step 2.4 — Account activation:** standard processing takes around 30
   minutes. You will receive an activation confirmation email from
   `access.management@campus-it.com` once your profile is live.

## 3. Critical Access & IT Support Contacts

- **Password resets & account lockouts:** Global Campus IT Service Desk — chat at
  `it-helpdesk.basel-campus.internal` or extension `*4433`.
- **Hardware issues (curved monitors, headsets):** IT Tech Bar, ground floor of
  the Primary Lab Block, open 09:00–12:00 and 13:00–16:00.

## 4. Mandatory Training Matrix for Laboratory Scientists

| Training program | Target audience | Mandatory deadline | Renewal cycle | Platform |
|------------------|-----------------|--------------------|---------------|----------|
| Tier L1–L3 Containment & Bio-Safety | All wet-lab researchers and molecular biology teams | Before Day 3 (prerequisite for physical lab badge activation) | Annual | EduNexus Portal |
| Data Integrity & ALCOA+ Research Principles | All scientists authoring entries in ELN Cloud | Within 14 days of join date | One-time | ComplianceHub |
| Chemical Hygiene & Volatile Solvent Transport | Any chemist handling hazardous or halogenated solvents | Within 7 days of join date | Every 2 years | EduNexus Portal |
| Advanced Procurement & Inventory Systems | All staff placing orders via ChemVantage or NexusBuy | Within 5 days of join date | One-time | SLS Learning Suite |
| Automated Conveyor Safety & Weight Logistics | All personnel using the vertical return networks | Within 10 days of join date | Every 3 years | SafetyPortal |
| Intellectual Property & Secure Data Sharing | All global employees handling pre-clinical data | Within 30 days of join date | Annual | InfoSec Portal |

## 5. First 30 Days Roadmap

| Timeline | Milestone | Required action | Verification owner |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|--------------------|
| Day 1 | Physical badging & IT asset retrieval | Collect tech bundle (headset, mouse, keyboard) from the IT Tech Bar; secure personal ID card for Node A & D access. | Campus Security Desk |
| Day 2 | Profile provisioning | Log into the UAP (`uap.basel-campus.internal`) and submit mandatory requests for BioLIMS v4 and ELN Cloud. | Lead Data Integrity Officer |
| Day 5 | Supervised bench walkthrough | Physical tour of the assigned Floor Logistics Zone to learn consumable placement and emergency spill kit locations. | Area Zone Manager |
| Week 2 | First independent assay validation | Execute a baseline sample-prep protocol under direct oversight of a senior chemist to verify instrument compliance. | Laboratory Supervisor |
| Day 30 | 30-day competency review | Complete the post-onboarding questionnaire in PeopleHub and log a 1-on-1 operational alignment meeting with the Lab Manager. | Department Line Manager |
