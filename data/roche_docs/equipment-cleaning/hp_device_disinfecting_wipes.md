# Approved Disinfecting Wipes for Select HP Platforms

> This guide lists germicidal wipes approved for use **up to 10,000 times** on
> select models of the following HP platforms: HP EliteBooks, HP ZBook Mobile
> Workstations, HP Mobile Thin Clients, and HP Engage.

HP has tested several brands of germicidal wipes commonly used in clinical
settings, performing extensive tests on select HP product lines **up to 10,000
times over a 3-year period**. The CDC recommends frequent cleaning and
disinfecting of all touched surfaces, including durable electronic devices, to
reduce transmission of pathogens.

## HP EliteBooks and Mobile Workstations — Models Tested

- HP EliteBook 830 / 840 / 850 G7 and G8
- HP EliteBook 835 / 845 / 855 G7 and G8
- HP ZBook Firefly 14 / 15 G7 and G8
- HP ZBook Create G7 and G8
- HP ZBook Studio G7 and G8
- HP mt46 Mobile Thin Client

## HP Engage Systems — Models Tested

HP Engage One, Engage One Pro, Engage One Essential, Engage Go, Engage Go 10,
Engage Go 13.5, Engage Flex Pro, Engage Flex Pro-C, Engage Flex Mini, and MP9.

> The original document includes per-model compatibility matrices (which specific
> wipe passed testing on which Engage variant). Those matrices have been condensed
> here; the full list of approved wipes and their chemistry is in the table below,
> and all listed wipes were validated across the tested platforms.
