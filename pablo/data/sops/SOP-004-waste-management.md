# Laboratory Waste Management Guide

This document defines the standardized procedures for material handling,
containment categories, and internal transport routing for biological and
chemical laboratory waste across all Industrial Containment Tiers (Tiers L1, L2,
and L3) within the facility.

> ⚠️ **Operational Notice:** The final operational division of labor regarding the
> transport of classified material from the localized primary floor zones to the
> secondary deep-processing decontamination facilities is undergoing a quarterly
> governance review. The current interim responsibilities are detailed in the
> master directive matrix below.

## Waste Handling Matrix

| Waste type | Containment tier | Primary containment & collection | Transfer responsibility | Secondary processing location |
|------------|------------------|----------------------------------|-------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Standard non-hazardous bio-waste | Tier L1 (Low impact) | Translucent eco-bags placed inside the designated floor sorting bays. | Assigned Floor Technician | Ground Floor Consolidation Loading Dock |
| Contaminated liquid & solid cultures | Tier L2 (Moderate isolation) | Hermetically sealed red canisters with integrated pressure-vent valves. | Area EHS (Environmental Health & Safety) Officer | Sublevel 2 (B2) Central Autoclave Suite |
| High-risk infectious biomaterials | Tier L3 (High containment) | Double-lined reinforced purple hazard drums with tamper-evident seals. | Certified Hazardous Materials Specialist | Specialized High-Containment Decontamination Facility (Wing C) |
| Sharps & broken glassware | All tiers (L1–L3) | Rigid, puncture-proof yellow poly-bins mounted to mobile laboratory benches. | Individual Performing Researcher | Material Recovery & Recycling Depot (Basement B1) |
| Volatile halogenated organic solvents | Tier L1 & L2 | High-density fluorinated amber carboys stored inside ventilated safety cabinets. | Campus Chemical Logistics Sourcing Team | Specialized Hazardous Waste Vault (Sublevel 1) |
