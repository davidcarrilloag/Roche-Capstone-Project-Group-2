# Ordering Chemicals, Life Science Products, and Consumables

## 1. Standard Material Supply on Lab Floors

The campus centralized logistics team distributes standard inventory and
high-frequency materials directly to your assigned floor. Commonly used lab
consumables are stocked inside the localized **Floor Logistics Zone**, located
only a few steps from your active workbenches, and are immediately ready for use.

If you need specialized materials or reagents that are not stocked in your
floor's logistics zone, follow the procurement procedure below.

## 2. Digital Procurement Suite

Log your request through the appropriate platform in the campus unified digital
workspace:

- **ChemVantage** — the platform for chemical and reagent inventory. Use it
  **exclusively** to order chemicals, synthetic compounds, antibodies, and life
  science biological products. It automatically links to global Safety Data
  Sheets (SDS).
- **NexusBuy** — the central campus procurement portal. Use it to order
  non-chemical lab consumables: pipette tips, Petri dishes, nitrile gloves,
  glassware, and general lab stationery.
- **LabLend & Go** — the self-service and asset-sharing application. Use it to
  buy immediate items from the on-site Lab Shop, access QR-coded stockroom
  materials, or borrow temporary equipment (e.g. calibrated sensors or portable
  vacuum pumps).

### Delivery rules & timelines (Automated Conveyor System)

- **Same-day cutoff:** submit digital orders no later than **13:00** to
  guarantee same-day delivery via the internal automated vertical conveyor
  network.
- **Notification:** you receive an automated routing email from the Central
  Supply Hub (`supplyhub@logistics.com`) once your items are packed and
  dispatched.
- **Conveyor windows:** the network operates **08:00–16:00**. Ambient-temperature
  items and standard chilled reagents (+4 °C) are routed inside a secure conveyor
  bin directly to your floor's logistics zone for pickup.

## 3. Specialized and Temperature-Sensitive Logistics

Certain compound classes, hazardous materials, or high-sensitivity products
**cannot** be sent via the automated conveyor network due to safety regulations.

The following items must be retrieved **in person** at the **Central Supply
Counter**, located on the ground floor of the Main Laboratory Wing:

- **Items requiring manual pickup:** deep-frozen compounds (−30 °C to −75 °C),
  specialized life science matrices, compressed gas cylinders, and strictly
  regulated controlled chemical precursors.
- **Counter opening hours:** Monday–Friday, **07:30–11:00** and **12:30–16:15**
  (split shift).

## 4. Custom Procurement Requests

For special bulk quantities, non-catalog custom items, or highly specialized
experimental synthesis requests that cannot be mapped within ChemVantage or
NexusBuy, submit a detailed technical specification list directly to the
sourcing team at `supplyhub@logistics.com`.
